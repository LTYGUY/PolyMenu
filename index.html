Introduction

Polymenu was established in 1990, and we have over 12 restaurants in Singapore. We serve everything, from ice creams to steaks. We want more people to experience our huge variety of flavourful foods. Try and have a taste, we promise it will be worth every cent!

Our Chefs' Experience

We send our chefs around the world, to have them taste all kinds of foods, for them to cook the best dishes for you!

Our duty

We strongly believe that people must eat good food to live a better life, make correct life choices, and be happier in general. Our food will remove any clouds in your mind, and so, it is our duty to make sure our food is delivered into your stomach.

First press 'Sign up'.

Read the given fields and provide your information. Once done, press 'Register'.

You will be prompted 'Registration success' at the top if you successfully registered. If so, you can click 'Sign in' to go to the login page.

First go to the login page by pressing the 'Login' button on top of the page. Then key in your login details press the green 'Login' button.

After you successfully login, you can then press 'Edit'.

You can then edit your account information.

After you are done with editing, press 'Confirm' to save.

You should be brought back to the account page, and you should see your account information updated!

You can only submit your order if you are logged in!


Press 'PolyMenu' to access the order page.



This is the order page. You can choose categories of foods, and also key in letters to search for them.




For this example, you can press 'Side' to access Sides category of foods.



For this example, you can key in 'soup' into the search bar, and it will return you food names that contains 'soup'.



You can then press 'Add to cart' to add to cart, it should feedback on the top main button 'Cart'.



You can add as many as you like, for this example, i add 3 Mushroom Soup in to cart. You can then click on the top button 'Cart' to go to the cart page.



In the cart page, you can choose to Add or Remove the foods you have added to cart. In this example I will remove 1 Mushroom Soup.



You can then press 'Clear Cart' to clear your cart, or 'Submit Order' to submit your order. In this example, I will submit order.



Once submitted successfully, it will inform you and clear your cart. You can then go to your account page to check out your order history.



You can see your order history at the most bottom part of your account information page.
